About Us Discover Our Story

Sensation is one of the oldest restaurant in Rome that’s been active since 1899 when our Enzo Cartieri and his best friend Luca Malfora built up this place from the ground up. Situated in a daydream scenario right in front of Trevi Fountain, inside Rome’s hearth itself. Nowadays is still one of the reference point for the indisputable Italian cuisine.
